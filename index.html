Welcome to BETA TSA

We're Learning to Lead in a Technical World.
Technology and Engineering Education

At BETA, a program called the "Technology Track" is offered. In it, students are able to take classes such as Principles of Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Digital Interactive Media, IB Computer Science, and more. You can learn more about our classes here and our teachers here.

Competition

At BETA TSA, we offer students the opportunity to compete in everything from Coding to Children's Storybook. There's an event for everyone and for all interests, and we're proud of all that our members have accomplished. You can learn more about general TSA here and more about our chapter in competition here.

Growth and Leadership

BETA TSA is active in the Rio Grande Valley community, and works to provide opportunities for students to volunteer and take on leadership positions at the regional, state, and national level. You can learn more about our student leaders here and our community involvement here.
