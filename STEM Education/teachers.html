BETA Technology and
Engineering Teachers

The educators of BETA's Technology & Engineering Program are dedicated to their profession and to their students. Each teacher brings a variety of experiences and skills to the department in order to help students be able to explore their interests and pursue their passions.
Vickie Rogé

Web Technologies, Web Design, Computer Maintenance

Vickie Rogè

Vickie Rogè is from Ft. Gibson, Oklahoma, and currently resides in Edinburg, Texas. She has been teaching for a total of 16 years, 10 of those years being at BETA. She received her Bachelor's in Business Education from Northeastern State University and her Master of Business Admistration Degree from the University of Texas Pan-American. She currently teaches Computer Maintenance, Web Design, and Web Technologies. Outside of school, she loves playing with new technology gadgets, reading, and spending time with her family.

Mrs. Rogè can be contacted at vickie.roge@stisd.net or at (956) 383-1684 ext. 8558

Alex Garcia

Pre-AP, AP, and IB Computer Science, Computer Science III

Alex Garcia

Alejandro "Alex" Garcia is from McAllen, TX. He has been an educator for 10 years, all of them spent at BETA. He has his Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from the University of Texas Pan-American, and currently teaches all of the Computer Science courses BETA offers (Pre-AP, AP, IB, and III). Outside of class, he enjoys reading, running, and watching Netflix. Mr. Garcia is also a BETA TSA Advisor and Texas TSA's State Conference Director.

Mr. Garcia can be contacted at alejandro.garcia@stisd.net or at (956) 383-1684 ext. 8557
