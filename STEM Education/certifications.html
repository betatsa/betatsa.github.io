Technology and Engineering Certifications

>>>>>>> origin/master
BETA offers a variety of certifications to its technology students that test the student's abilities in various skills and prepare them for a career in STEM at no cost to the student. Certifications offered include Adobe Certified Associate, Comp TIA Authorized Academy, Microsoft Office Specialist, and others. BETA is one of the few schools in its area to offer these certifications to its students.
Adobe Certified Associate
Adobe Certified Associate

The ACA certification allows you to demonstrate proficiency in Adobe digital communications tools. It is offered to students who are enrolled in Digital Interactive Media, Animation, or Web Design. Certifications are offered in Adobe Photoshop, Flash, Dreamweaver, Illustrator, Premier, and InDesign.

AutoDesk Certified
User
AutoDesk Certified User

The AutoDesk Certified User certification confirms students have the skills necessary to continue their design careers in 2D and 3D design skills. ACU is offered to students in the Principles of Engineering and Civil Engineering and Architecture classes. Certificatons are offered in Autodesk Revit, 3DS Max, AutoCAD, Inventor, and Maya.

CIW Web Foundations Associate
CIW Web Foundations Associate

The CIW certification makes sure that students have proven their understanding of important technologies that affect virtually every business. They are tested on networks, web basics, and working with a team in a business. The CIW certification is offered to students enrolled in the Web Technologies course.

Comp TIA A+ Certification
Comp TIA A+ Certification

The A+ certification validates understanding of hardware and software technologies and certifies the skills necessary to support IT infrastructures. CompTIA A+ is a useful credential that sets IT professionals apart in the work force. This certification is offered to students in the Computer Maintenance class.
